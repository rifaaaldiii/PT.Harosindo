About Us

PT Harosindo Teknologi Indonesia is a company engaged in metal stamping components. Established since 2015, with more than 150 employees. PT Harosindo Teknologi Indonesia has ISO 9001 certification in 2015 on quality management. With more than 7 years of experience, we are trusted to provide your needs in the field of metal stamping components.
